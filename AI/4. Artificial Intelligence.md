## AI
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the class of computational systems designed to perceive, learn from data, reason under uncertainty, and take actions to achieve specified objectives without being explicitly programmed for every scenario.

### Types of Artificial Intelligence

#### Narrow AI 
It is the type of AI that is designed to perform a specific task or a limited range of tasks. It works within a predefined set of rules and cannot go beyond its programming. Narrow AI does not possess true understanding or consciousness; it simply follows patterns and data it has been trained on. Most AI systems we use today, such as voice assistants, recommendation systems, and chatbots, belong to this category.

#### General AI (Artificial General Intelligence or AGI) 
It is a theoretical form of AI that would have intelligence similar to humans. It would be capable of learning, understanding, and applying knowledge across a wide variety of tasks, rather than being limited to one function. AGI would be able to reason, solve problems, and adapt to new situations just like a human being. However, this type of AI does not currently exist and remains a goal of future research.

#### Super AI (Artificial Super Intelligence or ASI) 
It is a hypothetical form of AI that would surpass human intelligence in all aspects, including creativity, decision-making, and problem-solving. It would be far more advanced than the smartest human minds and could potentially improve itself continuously. ASI is purely theoretical at this stage and is often discussed in science fiction and future technology debates.
