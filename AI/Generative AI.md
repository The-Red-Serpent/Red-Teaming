## Generative AI

Generative AI is a category of artificial intelligence that learns the underlying patterns and statistical distributions of training data and uses that learned knowledge to generate new, original content that did not previously exist but is statistically consistent with what it was trained on. Unlike traditional AI which takes existing data and produces a decision, classification, or prediction about it, generative AI takes a condition or prompt and produces entirely new data text, images, audio, video, code, or any other modality — by sampling from the probability distribution it learned during training.
